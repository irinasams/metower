About Metropolitan Tower

Constructed in 1987, Metropolitan Tower is located steps from Central Park and the shopping and dining destinations of world-famous 5th Avenue. The building offers white glove service with full time doormen and concierge staff. The building also includes a private dining room on the 30th floor offering complimentary breakfast to residents and their guests. .

Building amenities at 146 West 57th Street include bike room, laundry in the building, and a swimming pool. A rooftop deck offers stunning views of the Manhattan skyline as well as Central Park. In addition, the building offers convenient access to the N, Q, R, F, B, D and E subways trains giving residents easy transportation options to anywhere in New York City.

Metropolitan Tower is walking distance to Columbus Circle, giving residents access to some of Midtown's most iconic destinations.
